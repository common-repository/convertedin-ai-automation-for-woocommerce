== Description ==

The ConvertedIn AI Automation for WooCommerce plugin is an innovative tool that helps online store owners streamline their business operations and increase their sales. With this plugin, you can automate various aspects of your WooCommerce store, such as email marketing, product recommendations, and personalized product search results. The plugin uses powerful artificial intelligence algorithms to analyze customer behavior, preferences, and purchase history, allowing you to offer personalized and relevant product suggestions to your customers. Additionally, the plugin provides advanced analytics and reporting features, enabling you to track your store's performance and make data-driven decisions. Whether you are a small business owner or a large enterprise, the ConvertedIn AI Automation for WooCommerce plugin can help you save time and boost your online sales.

== Installation ==

Initialization page

 Upon clicking `confirm and continue` the plugin will create an account on ConvertedIn platform using your WordPress user email or the email you chose. if you already have an account with the email you chose your store will be added to that email and your datasource is successfully added

 #### You can always login to your account using the WordPress Plugin

1. navigate to ConvertedIn tab in your WordPress Dashboard
2. Click `Login to ConvertedIn`
